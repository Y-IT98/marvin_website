Banff

My most recent trip was to Banff. Went snowboarding here for the first time in almost 14 years of living in Canada.

Spent a few days here exploring the mountains for the most part. The view from the top at night was so good my friends had a hard time believing these photos weren't edited.

If you haven't been, I would highly recommend you visit, even it was just for a few days like I did.
